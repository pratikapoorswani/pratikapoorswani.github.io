About Me Who I am

Graphic Design Portfolio

Graphic design will save the world right after rock and roll does. - David Carson

An inquisitive, driven and creative 21 year old Graphic Designer and Art Historian.

​ I've spent my early design days studying at Srishti Institute of Art, Design, and Technology in Pune, India. My passion for design is much more than sitting at a computer and making ugly things look beautiful. I am always hungry for a challenge and am willing to go the extra mile to prove that I am a worthy designer. My designs come from the art that inspires me, for I believe that art has the power to transform, illuminate, educate, and motivate.

​ Great design is born of two things - simplicity and clarity.

​ My love for art drove me to pursue a degree in Art History and Visual Culture studies at Richmond the American International University in London, United Kingdom. I have a keen eye for examining art and a great interest in the art market sector as well as analysis and research.

Soundsleep - The App Web / Application


There’s nothing quite like the feeling of dread and anxiety that comes with lying in bed, knowing you can’t fall asleep, especially if you know you have an early day.
Soundsleep lets you choose from a wide variety of natural noises, ranging from city sounds to waves crashing on the beach. The app also allows you to download new sounds or add existing ones from your music library.
Listen for as little or as long as you want by using the start/stop timer.
